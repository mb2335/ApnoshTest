Project Timeline

How Does it Work?

From initial consultation and design planning to development, testing, and final launch, we outline each phase with expected timeframes and key milestones. This transparent process ensures smooth collaboration, keeping you informed and involved at every stage to deliver a successful, on-time completion.

  • Week 1

    Consultation

    Confirm the scope of work, project milestones, and any client responsibilities

    Send the client a detailed project questionnaire or fill out in person (branding, functionality needs, design preferences, target audience, etc.).

  • Week 2

    Design Phase

    Design wireframes and mockups based on the questionnaire and project scope.

    Share wireframes with the client for feedback. Prepare final high-fidelity mockups for client approval.

  • Week 3

    Development Phase

    Present final mockups to the client. Gather approval or any additional feedback for last-minute changes.

    Convert the approved designs into a fully functional website.

  • Week 4

    Launch

    Push the fully developed website live on the client’s domain.

    Conduct a final review and testing of the site in a staging environment.
